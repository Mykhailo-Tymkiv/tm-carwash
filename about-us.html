ABOUT TM EXPRESS

TM EXPRESS Car wash is unique in that we are a “Flex” service car wash. What that means to you is that we are the only car wash you will ever need. At TM EXPRESS, our approach combines the latest car wash technology with the human touch to provide the best car wash experience possible.

We believe in making customer satisfaction a top priority. We are vehicle owners just like you and we understand that it can be a stressful experience to trust your vehicle with someone else. At TM EXPRESS, you can rest assured that we will treat your vehicle as if it was our own. We wash one vehicle at a time, so we can make sure each vehicle receives the best treatment possible.
